What to Expect

Meals and Lodging

All meals from Sunday dinner through Thursday breakfast are included. Vegetarian food is available. Lodging is 4 person to a bedroom with queen or bunk beds


Outdoor Activites

Cost for some activities like ziplining, Kayaking and Rock climbing will be seperate. Free time and group recreation time have been built into the schedule.

Buzz Group

This consists of 4 or 5 people group, will provide an excellent opportunity to be honest and open with yourself and each other as we reflect upon what you are learning each day.

Talent Night

Everyone is welcome to participate. Song, dance, music, poetry, drama, karaoke, or any other forms of talent can be performed individually or as a group.
